Gesuido

Gesuido (下水道 means sewerage in Japanese) is a rather traditional roguelike game for iPhone with 1 bit color (black and white) pixel art, set in the underground maze beneath Tokyo.

Currently in development ----> Dev blog (ja)

Story

One night, the six-year-old Japan's Cabinet Chief Onmyo Secretary Seishiro Abe had a dream of six giants rampaging in Tokyo city. The giants destroyed everything and everyone. They looked just like the robots deveploed by the American big Internet-related company Georg (German pronunciation: [ˈɡeɔʁk]). Seishiro believed that the apocalyptic nightmare was a prophecy and wanted to prevent it really happening. He gathered some assassins from various time and space using his own supernatural power, and then sent them to the Georg's laboratory in deep underground of Tokyo to destroy it.

Assassins

Ronin

A samurai warrior of medieval Japan. He was a member of the Forty-seven Ronin, but he withdrawed the team before the raid. He uses katanas and bows and can move rather fast.

Barbarian

A warrior from Atlantis. He has a strong body and is good at swords and axes. Since he knows ancient magic, he can identify some magic potions.

Programmer

A modern Japanease bussiness man for a software company. He lacks exercise and has no combat skills. He knows basics of PHP and Ruby.

I will add more assassins in the future updates.

Secrets of the Tokyo underground

The Georg's robots laboratory is situated at deep below the Shimbashi Station in Tokyo. The assassins have to survive several levels to reach the laboratory.

1. Sewerage
This sewer network, made during the Meiji era, forms a multi-layer structure. After it was abandoned in the 1960s, for a long time it was the secret destination of industrial waste products and discarded experimental animals.
2. Zoo
The "Shitano Zoo" (zoo below ground) was built underground with consideration for the feng shui balance with the Ueno Zoo.
3. Yakuza office
The Ikeda-gumi yakuza group, backed by money from the Buddhist new religion that boasts more believers than any other in Japan, contributes to Georg to find and get industrial spies and human experimentation subjects. This office is also made in a basement for the sake of defensing the underground robot laboratory, and has members who are armed with pistols and rifles and who are on the lookout for intruders.
4. Laboratory
Its purpose is not disclosed, but six types of humanoid robots are made here.
